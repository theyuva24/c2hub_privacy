Community Guidelines – C2 Club

C2 Club is a student-focused community built for learning, collaboration, networking, and healthy discussions.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all users with respect. Disagreements are allowed, but harassment, bullying, insults, or personal attacks are not.

2. No Hate or Harassment

Hate speech, threats of violence, and repeated unwanted contact are strictly prohibited.

3. Use Anonymity Responsibly

Do not use anonymous features to spread hate, rumors, or false accusations.

4. Authentic Content

Do not post misleading, false, or manipulated information. Impersonation is not permitted.

5. Hub Participation

Follow both platform-wide guidelines and rules established by specific hub administrators.
