How to Delete Your Account

We respect your privacy and provide a simple way to delete your account and associated data.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the C2 Club app:

  • Open C2 Club on your device.
  • Go to your Profile.
  • Tap the Settings icon (top right).
  • Select Delete Account.
  • Confirm your choice. Your account will be queued for deletion immediately.

Option 2: Web Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can use our web form:

Go to Data Deletion Form →

What happens to my data?

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information, phone number, and private data are permanently removed.
  • Your authentication sessions are invalidated.
  • Public posts and comments are either deleted or anonymized based on your choice during deletion.
  • Data is purged from our active systems within 30 days.
